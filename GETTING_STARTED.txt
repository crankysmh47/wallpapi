Wallpapi - Getting Started
==========================

1. Open PowerShell in this folder.

2. Run:
     Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope Process -ExecutionPolicy Bypass
     .\install.ps1

3. Accept the license agreement.

4. Open a NEW terminal window.

5. Run:
     wp-cli status
     wp-ui

The wallpaper engine starts automatically at login.

Add your own videos or images to:
  %LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\Wallpapi\wallpapers

Uninstall:
  %LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\Wallpapi\uninstall.ps1

Documentation: README.md and docs\INSTALL.md
